BCA(Bachelor of Computer Application)

Vallabh Government College Mandi established in 1948, and BCA programme started in 2001. Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) is an undergraduate degree programme in computer applications. With the rapid growth of IT industry in India and worldwide, the demand of computer professional is increasing day by day. This increasing growth of IT industry has created a lot of opportunities for the computer graduates. BCA is one of the popular course among the students who want to make their career in the IT (information Technology) field and wants to work in the IT sector as programmer or software developer. BCA is a three-year full-time degree course divided across six semesters. Admission to the BCA degree of HPU is based on both merit and entrance. To be eligible for the admissions, candidates must have to complete their 10+2 from a recognized board with at least 40% marks as an aggregate.
